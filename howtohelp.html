How To Help

There are many ways to fight against gender inequality. Here's what you can do:

  • Let your voice be heard -- take a stand!
  • Donate to companies that have taken a stance
  • Sponser a child that needs your help
  • Be an activist -- raise awareness!
  • Get involved in reforms around the world
  • Tell your local senators to join the fight!
